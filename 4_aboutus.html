ABOUT US

KRUX is the heart of all things K-entertainment. We discuss topics regarding celebrities, lifetyle, and South Korea. Unable to keep up with so many things going on in social media? Fret not! We have weekly newsletters where we recap on recent happenings in the South Korean entertainment industry. Crunch for time? We also have Kapsules for quick reads and fun quizzes!
